# Market Sizing

Methodology for calculating Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM).

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| TAM | Total revenue opportunity if 100% market share achieved globally |
| SAM | Portion of TAM targetable with current product/service and business model |
| SOM | Realistic market share achievable within specific timeframe (typically 1-3 years) |

## Calculation Approaches

### Top-Down Approach

| Step | Formula | Data Source |
|------|---------|-------------|
| TAM | `Industry Revenue × Geographic Scope` | Industry reports, government statistics |
| SAM | `TAM × Segment Percentage × Geographic Filter` | Market research, demographic data |
| SOM | `SAM × Realistic Capture Rate` | Competitor analysis, growth benchmarks |

### Bottom-Up Approach

| Step | Formula | Data Source |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Unit Price | Average selling price per unit/transaction | Pricing research, competitor pricing |
| Potential Customers | Number of potential buyers in target segment | Census, industry data |
| TAM | `Unit Price × Total Potential Customers × Purchase Frequency` | Calculated |
| SAM | `TAM × Reachable Segment %` | Distribution capability, channel access |
| SOM | `SAM × Expected Penetration Rate` | Sales capacity, marketing reach |

## Market Sizing Variables

| Variable | Description | Typical Range |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Penetration Rate | % of SAM captured in Year 1 | 1-5% (new market), 5-15% (established) |
| Growth Rate | Annual market expansion | Varies by industry |
| CAGR | Compound Annual Growth Rate | Industry benchmark |

## Output Format

```
TAM: $X billion (Global/Regional)
SAM: $X million (Target Segment)
SOM: $X million (Year 1-3 Target)
Market Growth: X% CAGR
```
